The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

My all-time favorite video game. No other video game had ever gotten me as hooked as Tears of the Kingdom. Amazing map, story, graphics, combat, sound effects, music, and so much more. Exploring the world and discovering each weapon, monster, and going to new regions just gave me the greatest feeling. The game just makes exploration so fun to do too.

if i had dementia would experience again

Rating: 98/100

Terraria

A very fun game with cool bossfights, mechanics, progression, and the best soundtracks you will ever hear.

Just progressing through the game and collecting and crafting weapons, armor, and accessories was fun to me. Trying out different classes is also fun, like playing the game in a different way.

And playing with mods just enhances the game experience even more. I like playing the Calamity Mod with addons and a ton of QOL mods. Very coool.

Playtime: 363.9hrs
Rating: 89/100

Bloons Tower Defense 6

I think tower defense games are pretty fun to play. BTD6 is one of those TD games. NinjaKiwi are just great developers. They actually listen to the community.

BTD6 is pretty fun, with all sorts of challenges, strategies, and multiple units to place down. It just actually makes you think about what your next action should be.

Playtime: 115.5hs
Rating: 78/100

Minecraft

I played Minecraft a lot when I was little and watched content creators like DanTDM, PopularMMOS, and ExplodingTNT. I would just spend hours building in creative mode and sometimes even play with my friends and still have so many memories.

Overall I think the game can still be pretty interesting and progression and building can feel very achieving. But I think much of the game's enjoyment for me now lies in mods or multiplayer.

Rating: 81/100

Geometry Dash

I've spent way too long playing this game but it's pretty fun to practice, play and beat a difficult level. It was also cool to see how much I improved since I started playing and to actually start playing levels I thought I could never beat.

Also flexing on little children playing easy levels on the free versions is cool and pretty funny. Hardest level I beat was 'Acu' by Neigefeu.

Playtime: 797hrs
Rating: 72/100

The Battle Cats (EN)

This game has so much content for a free mobile game. No ads forced on you, and actual free-to-play gameplay. An absolute luxury nowadays.

This game is super fun to play with hundreds of 'cats' to collect and level up. It's also nice that there isn't just one super overpowered cat and that each cat actually has their own uses and shine on their own stages.

Playtime: 231hrs
Rating: 83/100

Pokemon Legends: Arceus

Arceus combines the open-world style of Breath of the Wild/Tears of the Kingdom and Pokemon, which I liked more than the traditional Pokemon games with unskippable battles against NPCs in the middle of your route.

The graphics aren't the best though and NPCs don't have any voice acting at all, but I didn't find that as too much of a problem with the interesting Pokemon catching system and gameplay itself.

Playtime: 25hrs
Rating: 71/100

Roblox

But there's always the occasional game with only a few thousand players and real passionate developers which I do enjoy now.
